The CEO

Nicolai Jensen | Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Aviation has never been just a profession — it has been a lifelong passion, shaped by dedication, experience, and a deep respect for the world of flight.

Meet Nicolai Jensen

From an early age, aviation captured Nicolai Jensen's imagination. What began as a childhood fascination with aircraft and flight developed into a lifelong dedication to aviation, airline operations, and the culture that surrounds professional flying.

With decades of aviation experience and a professional background as an airline captain, Nicolai brings a strong understanding of flight operations, safety, discipline, and the standards expected within a professional aviation environment.

Having lived across different parts of the world, including Scandinavia and South Africa, he brings an international perspective to Orange Tail Virtual Airline — combining European structure with global ambition and a strong passion for realistic virtual aviation.

Orange Tail Virtual Airline was created to be more than just another virtual airline. It was built to represent quality, pride, professionalism, and a serious love for aviation.

Professional Highlights

  • Aviation enthusiast for over 35 years
  • Professional airline captain
  • Founder of Orange Tail Virtual Airline
  • Strong focus on realism and operational standards
  • Passionate about airline branding and fleet identity
  • International aviation perspective from Europe and South Africa
