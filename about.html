Who We Are

Orange Tail Virtual Airline is built for pilots who believe virtual aviation should feel professional, realistic, stylish, and meaningful.

About Orange Tail Virtual Airline

Orange Tail Virtual Airline was created with one clear purpose: to offer a professional and immersive virtual airline experience for flight simulation pilots who value realism, quality, and pride in every operation.

We are not just a name on a schedule. We are a virtual airline built around strong branding, carefully planned routes, a respected fleet, and a clear standard of professionalism. From short regional flights to long-haul international operations, every Orange Tail flight is part of a larger aviation experience.

Our airline combines the visual identity of a modern carrier with the structure and discipline expected from serious flight simulation. We believe that a virtual airline should look good, operate properly, and give its pilots a reason to be proud of the aircraft they fly.

Our Mission

To create a premium virtual airline environment where pilots can fly with purpose, professionalism, and enjoyment — while representing a brand that looks and feels like a real-world airline.

Professionalism

We aim to maintain high standards in our operations, presentation, pilot conduct, and overall airline identity.

Realism

Our routes, fleet choices, and operational structure are designed to give pilots a believable and enjoyable airline experience.

Pride

Orange Tail is built to be an airline pilots are proud to represent every time they connect to the network and take to the skies.
